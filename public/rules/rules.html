Rules of the game

The objective of the game is to be the first one to reach 10.000 points and thereby declare themself the winner

The game

The game is played with 6 normal dice and some way of keeping track of points

Scoring:

The point scoring system is pretty simple and should be easy to understand and learn.
The following is true:

  1. Single 5's is worth 50 points
  2. Single 1's is worth 100 points
  3. Three of one kind is worth eyes * 100 (Three 2's = 200) except 1's which is worth 1000 points
  4. Three+ of a kind are rolled, each additional die is worth as much again as the three of a kind score (Three 4's = 400, Four 4's = 400 + 400)
  5. All numbers from 1 to 6 gives 1500 points
  6. Three pairs is worth 1000 points (2+2,3+3,5+5)
  7. SPECIAL RULE: If a player roll all 6 of a kind, (in one roll) its an automatic win

Winning:

Option 1: The first player to reach 10.000 is declared the temporary winner, as each player get 1 turn to get over that score.
Option 2: The winner have to reach exactely 10.000 points
Each player not at 10.000 points, will only get 1 extra turn, even if another player beats the temporary winners score.
